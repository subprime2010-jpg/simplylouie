Still have questions?

Do I need to give my phone number?

Telegram requires a phone number to sign up, but SimplyLouie doesn't ask for it or store it. We only see your Telegram username.

Will Louie understand my language?

Louie understands English well, plus Tagalog, Bahasa Indonesia, Swahili, and more. You can even mix languages — Louie will figure it out.

Is my conversation private?

Yes. Your conversations are between you and Louie. We don't share or sell them to anyone.

What happens after 7 days?

Louie will gently ask if you'd like to continue for $2/month. If you say no, your access pauses — no hard feelings.

What if I have slow internet?

Telegram is designed for slow connections. Louie responds with text, which uses very little data. It works even on 2G.
